Theodore Donald Kerabatsos aka Donny (played by Steve Buscemi) is the tertiary protagonist a friend of The Dude and Walter. He's an avid, incorrupt bowler and frustrates Walter who often says "Shut the fuck up Donny!". Donny dies of a heart attack in the movie, where as a result, we learn that his friends The Dude and Walter don't know much about Donny. At Donny's funeral, Walter says that Donny loves surfing.

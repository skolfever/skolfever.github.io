Jesus Quintana is the anti-villain a notable character in The Big Lebowski. He was born on February 28, 1950. He is an opponent of The Dude's bowling team and did six months in Chino for exposing himself to an 8 year old. He speaks with a thick Cuban American accent and refers to himself as "The Jesus". Walter calls him a "pederast". Jesus' bowling partner is Liam O'Brien, who is seen standing by Jesus when he confronts the protagonistic trio.
